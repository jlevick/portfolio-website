About

My Story

With 10 years in customer facing roles - from senior level administration to project coordination - and an educational background in anthropology and gender studies, I have honed my ability to solve problems with creativity, curiosity, and humility. I am an open, empathetic communicator who prefers to lift the figurative stone in every conversation, awed by what crawls out and the gems that always seem to be hidden underneath. My perserverance allows me to focus on quality completion, not credit or accolades. I believe failure is a friend and I don't shy away from asking questions. When I'm not immersed in Figma or poring over research, you'll find me climbing outside, dogs in tow.
